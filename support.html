Frequently Asked Questions

How do I identify a plant?

Tap the camera button on the main screen, take or upload a photo of the plant, and BloomIQ will identify it instantly.

How many free scans do I get?

New users receive 1 free scan. Subscribe to unlock unlimited plant identifications.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Go to your iPhone Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions → BloomIQ → Cancel Subscription.

My scan results seem incorrect

Plant identification accuracy depends on image quality. Try taking a clear, well-lit photo of the leaves, flowers, or distinctive features of the plant.
